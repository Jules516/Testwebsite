Policy

Environmental Policy

Toyo Seat Philippines Corporation, manufacturer of seats, automotive and non-automotive interior parts, is committed to conducting its business in an environmentally responsible manner.

To support this commitment, TSPC shall:

1. Incorporate environmental requirements in its planning activities and comply with all applicable environmental laws & regulations;

2. Minimize and/or eliminate the release of contaminants to the environment through optimization of energy and water use, and prevention of pollution by implementing our 6 environmental management programs:
• Energy Conversion • Waste Minimization
• Compliance to Clean Air Act • Water Conservation
• Waste Segregation • Fire Prevention

3. Continually review and improve the company's environmental management system.

Policy

Quality Policy

TOYO SEAT PHILIPPINES CORPORATION is committed to manufacturing automotive seats and interior parts that meet and exceed customer's expectations through reliable quality, on time delivery, and competitive cost.

We shall achieve this by:
1. Setting business targets that are customer oriented. 
2. Developing and implementing built in quality in processes and products.
3. Complying with applicable requirements. 
4. Proactively and systematically addressing issues in quality, delivery and cost competitiveness; and 
5. Driving continual improvement in all operations
