 Production System

Speedy without waste. Unique “one-stop production method”

Toyo Seat's "one-stop production system" can be said to be a characteristic of Toyo Seat that realizes just-in-time. Each process is arranged continuously on the same floor to eliminate stagnation between each process. By eliminating intermediate inventory and reducing waste, we are able to shorten delivery times and reduce costs.

 Quality Assurance

Indispensable quality assurance that continues to protect the attractiveness of the product

Quality assurance, which confirms the accuracy of products, is also an important part of Toyo Sheet's products. Through verification and evaluation activities for the degree of process assurance, we are creating a system that "does not receive, manufacture, or distribute defective products." We aim to create attractive products that exceed customer expectations.
