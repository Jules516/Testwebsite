Features of Toyo Seat's business

 Research and Development

R&D system to realize safety and comfort

Car seats and convertible tops that provide comfort and safety in all climate conditions around the world. We conduct research and development based on ergonomics and sensibility engineering, as well as tests under harsh environments such as crash tests and temperature loads. Toyo Seat's unique aesthetics, "SUWARU," which brings smiles to everyone's faces, runs through it.

 Industrial science

Simulate a flexible, high-quality production line

We are shortening the mass production lead time using a production simulator for process design for production. In addition, in order to improve work efficiency, we conduct production simulations to flexibly respond to various production fluctuations and contribute to the production of safe, secure, stable and inexpensive products.

 Production System

Speedy without waste. Unique “one-stop production method”

Toyo Seat's "one-stop production system" can be said to be a characteristic of Toyo Seat that realizes just-in-time. Each process is arranged continuously on the same floor to eliminate stagnation between each process. By eliminating intermediate inventory and reducing waste, we are able to shorten delivery times and reduce costs.

 Quality Assurance

Indispensable quality assurance that continues to protect the attractiveness of the product

Quality assurance, which confirms the accuracy of products, is also an important part of Toyo Sheet's products. Through verification and evaluation activities for the degree of process assurance, we are creating a system that "does not receive, manufacture, or distribute defective products." We aim to create attractive products that exceed customer expectations.
